FAQ / The Rules

How does this work?

This is a lookbook for my private group buy community. You see something you like, you reach out. I coordinate bulk orders every Friday with trusted suppliers to secure the best pricing for everyone. It's about quality, not quantity.

How do I pay?

50% cash deposit to secure your spot in the weekly order. Remainder due on delivery. All deposits are final since funds are immediately allocated to secure inventory. We accept cash, Venmo, or Zelle.

How long does it take?

Typically 7-10 business days after the Friday order placement. Some items may take longer depending on sourcing. I'll keep you updated with tracking and delivery estimates throughout the process.

What's "1:1 Rep" vs "Factory 2nd"?

"1:1 Rep" means high-quality replica with identical materials and build quality. "Factory 2nd" typically comes from the same production facility but didn't pass final QC for minor cosmetic flaws. Both offer excellent value and performance.

Do you offer any guarantees?

Yes - if any item fails within 30 days due to manufacturing defect, I'll coordinate a replacement through my supplier network. Digital services come with replacement guarantee if access is lost.
