The Protocol (FAQ)

How do I get a bottle?

This is just the catalog. Hit me up in person to make the deal. All pickups are face-to-face, keeps everything clean and simple. No shipping, no online transactions.

How good is the quality?

These aren't those weak knockoffs. Every bottle performs exactly like retail - same projection, same longevity, same compliments. I only work with suppliers who get it right.

What about batch codes and authenticity?

Every bottle has proper batch codes and passes authentication. These come from the same supply chains as authorized retailers, just through different channels.

Can I request specific fragrances?

Always down to source new heat if there's demand. Hit me up with what you're looking for and I'll see what I can make happen. Popular requests usually make it into the rotation.

What's your return policy?

If a bottle doesn't perform as expected, we'll make it right. That's why first-time buyers get samples - so you know exactly what you're getting before you commit to a full bottle.
